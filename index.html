Top Picks This Month

Shopping & Thrifting
Beyond Retro Vintage • Coal Drops Yard

Beyond Retro in Coal Drops Yard is a must-visit for vintage lovers, offering a huge selection of curated retro clothing, accessories, and unique statement pieces that capture London’s effortlessly cool style. Located in the heart of King’s Cross, the shop fits perfectly into Coal Drops Yard’s creative, industrial-chic setting.

Beyond Retro is just one part of what makes Coal Drops Yard special — the area blends independent boutiques, stylish restaurants, riverside bars, and design-led spaces within beautifully restored Victorian warehouses. Visitors can shop, eat, relax by the canal, or catch pop-up art installations and events, making it one of London’s trendiest spots to spend an afternoon or evening.

Food & Drink
The Bloomsbury Lunch Market • Torrington Square

The Bloomsbury Lunch Market, held on UCL’s campus at Torrington Square, is a lively weekday street food market that brings together some of London’s best global eats. From fresh bao buns and Greek gyros to vibrant vegan dishes and homemade desserts, it’s a favorite lunchtime spot for students, locals, and visitors alike.

People love it for its affordable, diverse food options, the friendly, energetic atmosphere, and its prime location right next to the British Museum — making it the perfect place to grab a bite before or after exploring one of London’s top cultural landmarks.

Art & Culture
The Wallace Collection • Founded: 1897

A stunning national museum housed in a historic townhouse near Marylebone, showcasing an extraordinary array of fine and decorative arts. Its galleries are filled with 18th-century French paintings, intricate armor, sculpture, and elegant furniture — all once part of a private aristocratic collection.

People visit for its intimate, elegant atmosphere (it feels more like exploring a grand home than a museum), the world-class art including works by Fragonard, Boucher, and Titian, and the beautiful courtyard café that makes it a perfect cultural escape in central London.
